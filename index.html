Who?

Toby Marks is a Perth (Whadjuk Boodjar) born web developer currently residing in Cordoba Capital, Argentina. In 2017 He started his journey into the world of web developmet/design. Initialy focusing on web design he was wisped away on the endless zepher that is web development. His time in the industry has taken him from the humble begginings of Content management services(CMS), html and css to the far more complex world of Javascript and it's many libraries and frameworks. He currently works with vanilla Javascript, React and Jquery. When he's not writing code or building websites he might be found playing with his four year old daughter, teaching english, sitting in a comfortable chair reading a book drinking yerba mate. Services

My services are aimed at helping creatives and small businesses get exposure and hold space in the digital realm while staying true to their real life identidies.

In the vast world of web services there is a plethora of options and it can get pretty complicted pretty quickly. I'm here to try and simplfy those for you and to help you navigate them and find out exactly what it is you want and custom build solutions that fit within your creative parameters. I want to display your work/products in the best way possible whilst giving you as much exposure as possible. I do this by using the latest web technologies, including but not limited to the following


  • Semantic and accesible HTML(code thats easy to read)
  • Cross browser and dynamic content friendly CSS(if it looks good on one browser it'll look good on them all)
  • Mobile and responsive design
  • SVG animations
  • Javascript(to create more dynamic and useful websites)
  • CMS intergration(for things like shopify, wordpress, etc)
